Explore Bushkill Falls

Just minutes from our hotel, discover the breathtaking beauty of Bushkill Falls, known as the "Niagara of Pennsylvania." This natural wonder features a series of eight cascading waterfalls, set against a backdrop of vibrant fall colors.

  • 4 hiking trails of varying difficulty
  • Scenic overlooks and bridges
  • Wildlife viewing opportunities
  • On-site mining maze for kids

Ask our front desk about discounted tickets and guided tours!
